St Paris Newspaper Abstract, June 19, 1890

PB Ross, President of Urbana Bank for 23 years, died Sunday, aged 78 years. He was courteous and reliable.

Urbana Newspaper Abstract, June 19, 1890

Mr PB Ross, well known and highly esteemed citizen of Urbana, died at his home Sunday. He was born the 12th October, 1812, in Clark County, children William R Ross of Urbana, Mrs Anna J David of Chicago, Charles A Ross, AP Ross, Mrs George A Talbott of Urbana. The first two are children by first wife; his second wife died some months ago.
