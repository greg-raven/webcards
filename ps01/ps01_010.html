Notes for Sophia Matilda FUELLING
Miss Fuelling Succumbs After Accident

Ill since she fell and broke her hip while alighting from a street car in Los Angeles about a week ago, Miss Sophie M. Fuelling, 72, succumbed from injuries and shock Tuesday night in the Hollywood hospital, where she had been since the accident.

Miss Fuelling, librarian for the Christian Science reading room here, had lived in Canoga Park 10 years. She was born in Baltimore, Md., December 6, 1866.

Christian Science services will be read Friday afternoon at 2 p.m. at Shierry & Walling chapel, followed by interment in Oakwood cemetery.

Miss Fuelling leaves a sister, Mrs. Helena F. Wilson of Redlands, and a brother, Edward Fuelling, with who she made her home at 7225 Alabama avenue.
