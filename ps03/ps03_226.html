Notes for Joseph Oliver MOORE
Joe and Amanda Moore lived in Stamford after 1900, where they were a highly esteemed couple. They celebrated their golden wedding anniversary in 1928. He had farm and ranch interests and was associated with the Stamford Compress Company. The family were active in the Methodist church and in various community enterprises. Their large family produced several professional men. Other members of the family are successful business men and prominent women in their communities.
