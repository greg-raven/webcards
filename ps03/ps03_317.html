Peter Martin Raven, 57, of Santa Fe, New Mexico, died Thursday, 20 August 1987.

Born on Tuesday, 24 June 1930, in Long Beach, Los Angeles County, California, he was the son of the late Jens Kristian Raven formerly of Inglewood, CA and Louise (Bendiksen) of England.

Peter Martin is survived by one son, Gary Wayne of Long Beach, Los Angeles County, California; one sister, Gloria Ann Coleman and her husband Richard Coleman of Long Beach, Los Angeles County, California.

Peter Martin was preceded in death by one daughter, Jennifer Jean of Escondido, California; four grandparents, Soren Martin Bendiksen of Wilmington, CA, Olga Kristine (Pedersen), UNKNOWN Husband Ravn, and UNKNOWN Wife (Christen).
