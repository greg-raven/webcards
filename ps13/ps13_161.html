Notes for Anna Katharina GERHARD
“Anna Katherine Stumpf Nee Gerhard was my 2 great-grandmother she was the daughter of Johann Heinrich Gerhard born 1766 Germany died 1843 Germany. Her mother was Anna Katherine Gerhard Nee Shleich born 1772 Germany died 1847 Germany. Anna married Johann Heinrich "Gonther" Stumpf born 1811 Germany died 1891 Ontario Canada. They married about 1832.

“Anna and Johann had the following children. Elizabeth Margaret born 1829 Germany. Anna Katherine Neeb Steel Nee Stumpf born 1833 Germany died Canada 1924 Marie Dorothy born 1834 Germany died Germany. Conrad Konrad born 1836 Germany died 1916 Los Angeles California. Johann Sylvester born 1840 Germany died 1925 Canada born 1841 Germany died 1844 Germany. Ann Katharina Catherine Schuele Nee Stumpf born 1847 Germany died 1908 Los Angeles California. Johann Heinrich "Hy" born 1850 Germany died 1910 Canada. Sophia born Germany 1853 died 1854 Germany. Peter born 1855 Germany died 1860 with his mother of Typhoid Fever Omaha Nebraska. They had gone to stay with relatives while her husband prepared their home in Canada.”
