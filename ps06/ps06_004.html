Notes for Johann KEIM
Arrived in America for the first time at Philadelphia in 1698. Returned to Europe, and came back to America with his new wife in 1707.

Left a written record of his life, the last entry of which is 1732.

Married 14 days before the festival of St. Michael (in September).

His estate was probated January 1, 1754, Reading Courthouse. The estate indicates he had 16 children.
