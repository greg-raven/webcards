Notes for William Terry LESTER
In 1850, William and Mary were farming in Dist. #15, Floyd Co, VA and their real estate value was $600.

In 1870, William and Mary were farming in Alum Ridge, Floyd Co, VA

In 1870, William's real estate value was $850 and his personal property value was $260.

In 1880, William and Mary were living in Alum Ridge, Floyd Co, VA and William was a Blacksmith.

William served as Captain of Militia prior to the Civil War, and was 2nd Lieutenant in the Forty-Second Reserves, under Col. Robert L. Preston in the Confederate army. (text obtained from "Floyd Co: A History of It's People & Places" By: Amos D. Wood)
