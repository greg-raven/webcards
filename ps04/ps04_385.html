Notes for Johann Leonard MOSER
Surry Co. wills I. 166a. Leonard MOSER 16 Jul 1782. Wife Sara gets 200 acres adj. Wachovia line on South line. Sons John & Peter get 200 acres adj. Wachovia line. Children: Jacob Moser, Michael Moser, Francis Moser, Samuel Moser, Christian Moser, Henry Moser, Sarah, Mary, Elizabeth, Christina. Son Leonard gets 1 sh sterl. Exrs: Adam Binkley, Am Woolf. Wit: Philip Daniel, Adam Woolf, Adam Lintales. Prvd by Adam Woolf. Rec. Aug. Ct. 1782.

It appears that some of his older children stayed in Maryland, but the younger ones moved with the family to NC.
