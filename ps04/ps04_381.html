Dates from Bill Holder, 8/96 (his ancestors)

Family group sheet from Carter L. Hilsabeck gives alternative birth dates of 9 Dec 1790 or 9 Apr 1791.

This family group sheet lists spouses for most of the children. Since they're all in Iowa or elsewhere in the midwest I have not included them here except for the ones whose surnames suggest an NC origin.

Another handwritten sheet sent by Bill Holder, from family records of Edna Hilsabeck Lane, daughter of Ed Hilsabeck, gives the Dec. 9, 1790 birth date, and gives full birthdates for all the children. This one gives marriage date as Aug. 14, 1813, which would be more consistent than 1814 (as I originally had it) with birth of Elias in Feb. 1815.
