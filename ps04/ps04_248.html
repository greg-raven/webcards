Notes for Frederick HOLDER
Marriage of Frederick Holder and Esther Leinbach is listed in Carmichael History, with note that Frederick's parentage is undetermined. The information is under the children of Joseph Holder and Johanna Maria Vollweiler, but he does not appear among their children christened at Shiloh.

This information was previously in a duplicate file for Frederick. Since the dates match with this son of George Holder, I am assuming they are one and the same, and have deleted the duplicate reference.

Frederick and Esther joined the congregation at Bethania in 1845 at the same time as George Krause, nephew of Wendel Krause, and his wife Louisa Holder, daughter of George and granddaughter of Joseph. Frederick was still a member of the Bethania congregation in 1852, but was buried at Shiloh, suggesting he may not have been a Moravian at the time of his death.
