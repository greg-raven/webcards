Notes for Dean L. HARGROVE
Obituary:

—

Dean L. Hargrove, dearly loved son, father, brother, uncle and devoted friend to many, passed away on Tuesday, February 1, 2005, in St. George, UT.

Dean was born in Los Angeles, CA on October 2, 1958. Dean was known to so many as the "guy with the big heart", there wasn't anything he wouldn't do for his family, friends and even strangers.

He liked to fish, hunt, and had an intense love of all types of music. Dean is survived by his parents, Karol and Bill Hargrove of Fresno; his children Dean Jr., David, and Kylie of Fresno; his sisters, Karen Hargrove Mohr and her husband Rick, Linda Hargrove Mackay and her husband Greg, and Johna Hargrove Wilson and her husband Jim; loving nieces, nephews, aunts, uncles and friends Jennifer, George, Jaime, Gary, John, Natalie, Lindsay, David, Garrett, Sydney, Griffin, Jil, Justin, Skyler, Savannah, Ashlynn and Jasmine; aunt Shirley Jones; uncle Ralph Hargrove; and L.A. County Sheriffs partner, Brian Flynn.

Visitation will be held at the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints Stake Center, in Clovis, CA on Monday, February 7, 2005, at 10:00 a.m.

A Funeral Service will be held at the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, 220 N. Peach Ave. in Clovis, CA on Monday, February 7, 2005, at 11:00 a.m.

Remembrances may be made in Dean's name to: The LDS General Mission Fund, c/o LDS Church Orchard View Ward, 156 W. Warwick, Clovis, CA 93619; The Well Community Church Youth Group, 620 W. Fairmont, Fresno, CA 93705; or The Salvation Army Adult Rehabilitation Center, 804 S. Parallel Ave., Fresno, CA 93721.
