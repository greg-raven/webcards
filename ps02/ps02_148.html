Janice E. (Messerly) Wilson, 86, of Escondido, California, died Friday, 9 April 1993.

Born on Wednesday, 18 July 1906, in Glendale, Los Angeles County, California, she was the daughter of the late Everett Alpheus Messerly formerly of Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, California and the late Emma Gertrude (Stumpf) formerly of Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, California.

On Friday, 24 May 1991 she was preceded in death by her husband of 65 years, Omar Jean Wilson, son of the late Herman Treadway Wilson formerly of Redlands, San Bernardino County, California and the late Helena (Fuelling) formerly of Costa Mesa, California.

Janice E. is survived by three children, Everett Richard and his wife Nancy Joanne (Dobie) of Glendale, Los Angeles County, California, Nancy Helena Martin of Glendale, Los Angeles County, California, and Victor and his wife Doris Suzanne (Keim) of Glendale, Los Angeles County, California; six grandchildren, Gregory Neil Raven and his wife Valerie Lynn (Varnell) of Hollywood, Los Angeles County, California, Gary Wayne Raven and his wife Lynanne (Tuttle) of Long Beach, Los Angeles County, California, Jeffrey Alan of Newport Beach, California, Dobie of Costa Mesa, California, Bruce Michael and his wife Dorothy Nicholes (Georgeoff) of Newport Beach, California, and Kenneth Bradford and his wife Angela Clare (Oberg) of Costa Mesa, California; four G grandchildren, Caden Jan Houson of Valley Center, California, Erika Eliza Jennifer (Raven) of Seattle, Washington, Hailey Ann (Houson) of Valley Center, California, and Heather Elizabeth of Bonsall, California.

Janice E. was preceded in death by two sisters, Mary Elizabeth (Messerly) of Vida, Oregon, and Catherine Eloise (Messerly) of Dublin, California; four grandparents, Conrad Stumpf of Kansas City, Jackson County, Missouri, Caroline Matilda (Wassman) Stumpf of Kansas City, Jackson County, Missouri, Alpheus Everett Messerly of Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, California, and Clara Elizabeth (Schaffer) Messerly of Los Angeles, Los Angeles County, California; one granddaughter, Jennifer Jean (Raven) of Escondido, California.
